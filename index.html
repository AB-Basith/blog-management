Who we are

About Us

Al Ihsan Brothers is a non-profit charitable organization based in Melapalayam, Tirunelveli. We are a group of dedicated individuals working together to serve humanity with compassion, inspired by Islamic values of kindness, generosity, and community support.

Our journey began with a simple goal: to extend a helping hand to families in need. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted support system for the underprivileged, focusing on widows, orphans, and struggling families. Through our various programs, we provide monthly groceries, education assistance, medical support, Ramadan food distribution, and Eid Qurbani services. We also conduct blood donation camps, awareness programs, and community development activities to create a stronger and healthier society. At Al Ihsan Brothers, we believe that small acts of kindness can create big changes. With the support of our members and well-wishers, we continue to work towards building a brighter, more caring future for all.

Our History

Al Ihsan Brothers was founded in 2020 in Melapalayam, Tirunelveli, by a group of like-minded individuals who believed in serving humanity with compassion. What began as a small initiative to provide groceries to a few families has now grown into a well-structured organization supporting hundreds of families every month. In our early days, our primary focus was monthly grocery distribution. Over time, we expanded our services to include medical support, orphan sponsorship, Ramadan food programs, Eid Qurbani services, and educational assistance. Our group has also been actively involved in community service projects such as blood donation camps, awareness campaigns, and knowledge-sharing initiatives. With Allah’s blessings and the continuous support of our donors and volunteers, we are committed to expanding our reach and helping more families in the years ahead.
